## v1.1 BACKLOG — quirk-hunt findings (6 parallel Playwright hunts on the live site)

Nothing is broken/crashing; honesty + data correctness verified solid. These are UX/layout/a11y polish. Top two clusters worth a fix pass first:

### Fix-first cluster
- **Attribution occlusion (licensing) — state/width-dependent.** Desktop legend (bottom-left) + ranked list (right) can slide over the CC BY 4.0 / OSM credit at some widths; one mobile state saw it covered, another saw it clear. The MapLibre compact credit appears wide enough at some widths to go under the side panels. Fix so the credit is NEVER occludable (it should always stay findable). Files: `site/src/styles/controls.css` (`--attrib-safe-bottom`), `site/src/map/init.ts` (AttributionControl compact config), legend/ranked/panel z-order + the 0.92 panel opacity (labels/attrib bleed through).
- **Marker interaction cluster:** (a) single click on a marker pill often does nothing — the composite pill is a DOM overlay under MapLibre's canvas hit-layer, so first taps land on the canvas; (b) the 2 sample stations physically overlap in the SW corner (Reykjavík nearly unclickable) — will self-resolve with the national dataset but the click-through is real; (c) markers vanish when zooming IN (0 pills at zoom 8/11) — confirm it's data sparsity vs a composite-renderer/queryRenderedFeatures cull bug. Files: `site/src/map/markers.ts` (hybrid symbol-collision + `#marker-overlay` composite, click delegation).
