## Phase Goal

**As a** visitor planning an Icelandic trip, **I want to** see stations colored by their combined weather score with a legend, a ranked best-stations list, and a plain explainer, **so that** I can tell at a glance where the weather has historically been best and trust how that score was reached.

Extend `computeMarkerDatum` to derive the rain-total mm, feed the three raw window-metrics into the domain component curves, call `combine()`, and surface `score: number | null` (+ `missingRain`) on `MarkerDatum`. The rain UNITS (mm window-total, not the `hasPrecip` boolean) are the one genuinely load-bearing assumption (RESEARCH A3) and MUST be pinned by a fixture test here.

Output: extended `MarkerDatum` type + `averages.ts`, `mutedDatum` score:null sync, `score-color.ts` + tests, extended `averages.test.ts`, and the `score.spec.ts` skeleton.
