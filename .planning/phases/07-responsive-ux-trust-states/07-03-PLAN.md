<objective>
The mobile responsiveness slice (UX-03, the headline deliverable). Fill the Plan 01 `attachSheet` stub with a matchMedia(640px)-gated Pointer-Events drag controller that promotes the Phase 6 `.station-panel` to a draggable, non-modal bottom sheet (peek ↔ expanded) on phones — the map stays pannable above it — while the desktop side panel is unchanged. Collapse the ranked list and legend to chips on mobile (only one station-focused surface expanded at a time), keep the control bar usable at 390px with no horizontal overflow, and reflow the attribution safe-zone above the sheet peek so the credit stays legible.

Purpose: Make mobile a first-class audience (trip planners browse on phones). Close UX-03 and pay down the last of the responsive layout debt. After this plan a phone user can tap a station, drag the sheet up to read the full chart stack, pan the map behind it, and reach every control — without horizontal overflow or an occluded credit.

Output: the `attachSheet` drag controller, bottom-sheet CSS geometry in panel.css, mobile chip collapse for ranked/legend, the `--attrib-safe-bottom` sheet reflow, and the activated responsive.spec criteria 1-5 (+ the mobile attribution assertions).
</objective>
